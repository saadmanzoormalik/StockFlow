# Notification Strategy

## Product Principle

The morning notification should not be generic news. It should point the user into the most important decision created by the app's algorithm and personalization layer.

## Morning Nudge Pattern

Notification:

> Your market briefing is ready. VRT still fits your profile. AI power demand strengthened, but no buy trigger yet.

In-app briefing:

1. Most important decision.
2. Model factor driving the decision.
3. What is missing or uncertain.
4. Balanced action: Buy, Watch, Avoid, Trim, or Learn.

## Balance Rule

Every briefing should include:

- one action,
- one risk,
- one missing signal,
- one learning opportunity.

This keeps the app useful without overwhelming users or encouraging impulsive trading.

## Engagement KPIs

- push open rate
- briefing completion rate
- top pick save rate
- dismiss rate
- portfolio connect rate
- risk update completion
- daily active use
- average session length
- action-to-outcome calibration

## Compliance Guardrail

Do not send notifications promising returns. Use decision-support framing:

- "watchlist"
- "briefing"
- "risk changed"
- "new catalyst"
- "your portfolio may be missing"
- "expected range changed"

Avoid:

- "buy now"
- "guaranteed"
- "earn X% by date"
- "can't miss"
